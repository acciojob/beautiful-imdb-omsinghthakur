Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan, who also wrote the screenplay. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Ken Watanabe, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Dileep Rao, Cillian Murphy, Tom Berenger, and Michael Caine. DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, a thief who extracts information from the subconscious mind of his targets while they are dreaming. His abilities have cost him his family and his freedom, but he gets a chance at redemption when he is offered the chance to plant an idea in someone's mind.
